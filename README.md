
**News App**

Stay informed with the latest news from around the world through a sleek, modern interface. This app connects to reliable APIs to bring you breaking stories, trending topics, and in-depth articles in real time.

Key Features:
*Powerful Search: Find news easily by keywords or topics.
*Flexible Reading: Open articles directly in the app or your browser.
*Beautiful Design: Enjoy a clean, intuitive layout for a seamless experience.
*Organized Categories: Browse news by categories for quick navigation.
*Get all the news that matters to you, anytime, anywhere.
